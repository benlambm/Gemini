Key Terms

Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) refers to a theoretical form of AI that possesses the ability to understand, learn, and apply knowledge across a wide range of tasks at a human-like level. Narrow AI describes the systems we use today, which are designed to perform specific tasks, such as generating text or identifying images, using Machine Learning (ML), a method where algorithms improve their performance by identifying patterns in vast datasets.
