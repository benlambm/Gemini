Discussion: AI in Media and Reality

Key Terms

Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)
Refers to a theoretical form of AI that possesses the ability to understand, learn, and apply knowledge across a wide range of tasks at a human-like level.
Narrow AI
Describes the systems we use today, which are designed to perform specific tasks, such as generating text or identifying images.
Machine Learning (ML)
A method where algorithms improve their performance by identifying patterns in vast datasets.
